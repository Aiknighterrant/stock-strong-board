=== 如何获取GitHub Token ===
1. 访问: https://github.com/settings/tokens
2. 点击: "Generate new token"
3. 选择: "classic" token
4. 权限: 勾选 "repo" (完全控制仓库)
5. 生成并复制token (格式: ghp_xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx)
